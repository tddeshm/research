I am an Undergraduate student at the University of Michigan studying Physics and Computer Science.

Currently, I am working on researching light commands through the SPQR lab. Light commands are attacks on MEMs microphones where attackers can inject commands into voice-activated systems such as Aexa, Siri or Google Assistant using laser-based audio injection. We are currently investigated the physics behind the attack by looking at consequences of the photoacoustic and photovoltaic effects.
